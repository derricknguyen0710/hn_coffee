Oriental Coffee

The Eastern and Oriental Tea House has moved a few buildings down from the former location. But, it's still beautiful and has really delicious food. It caters more to a foreign and affluent Vietnamese crowd but it's a very nice place to go. There is a counter on the second floor with a beautiful view of the lake as well as other tables scattered inside and out. The coffees are delicious (especially the coconut coffee) and the salads looked great too.

This cafe is one of the rare cafe in Hanoi that brings a nostalgic and cozy feelings for the customer. It would be a great place for family or couple who want to hangout and chill in the weekends and amazed by the unique decoration the cafe because of its exquisite combination of Eastern and Oriental cultures.
