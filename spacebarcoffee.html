SpaceBar Coffee

Spacebar is a charming cafe tucked down an alley off To Ngoc Van and a few steps away from West Lake. Whether you are looking for a quiet space with good internet and decent coffee (Oriberry, yum!) or some tasty, well-priced fare in the Tay Ho area, this is definitely a place you should check out.

Spacebar is located in one of the most quiet yet interesting alleys in Tay Ho. You can pick a seat either outside under the huge mango tree to enjoy fresh air or inside the building with cool pop-art design. I often come to Spacebar at the weekend either alone or with several friends and always find comfort and joy. The staff is nice and friendly. I love the recovery breakfast and breakfast roll served at Spacebar. You can work, hang out and relax at this Co-working cafe.
