Kadi Cafe

Kadi Cafe is not the coffee shop to choose if you’re looking for peace and quiet. Situated on the corner of a busy junction connecting with Yen Phu, Kadi is a place in which the beeping horns and whirring engines will be background noise. That being said, if you want to idle away the time watching traffic, there’s no better spot in Hanoi.

Given its location, the coffee shop serves as the perfect spot to attempt to understand the eccentricities of life on Hanoi’s roads. Its tiny bar area downstairs doesn’t look much, but head upstairs for a round space with ceiling to floor windows providing a 360 degree view of the organised chaos below.
