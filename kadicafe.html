Kadi Cafe

Kadi Cafe is a very nice coffee shop in Hanoi. The view and atmosphere is very relaxing and the staff are helpful and friendly. The prices are good, and the drinks are delicious. I recommend their coconut coffee very much! If you want to have a delicious coffee with friends or a loved one, this is the right place for it.
