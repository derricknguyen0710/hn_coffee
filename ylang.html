The YLang Gardenista

Make a break from the rows of glammed-up mannequins, though, and you’ll find a serene retreat on the corner of Van Bao. With outdoor umbrella-shaded tables on the right and a flower-filled greenhouse on the left, Gardenista has all the allure of a botanical garden under the guise of a café.

Flower-pots, hanging plants and terrariums fill every gap and surface, successfully meeting the quota of ‘green’ needed in a greenhouse. Staff intermittently squirt plants with water and miniature succulents creep onto every table, taking precedence over cutlery.
