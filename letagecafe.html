L'ETAGE CAFE

Obviously the goal here is to achieve a spot on the balcony, which overlooks Hoan Kiem’s loveliest and leafiest corner. This slender platform has been provided with a few wooden bar stools which most people can’t stand for too long. Deliberately uncomfortable or not, these highchairs work well to ensure a steady rotation of customers and a chance for everyone to enjoy the primary reason they came.

All the juices are made with fresh fruit so don’t be disappointed if they are out of stock or your choice isn’t in season. The egg cocoa is superb, the egg coffee has a brilliant reputation, and the addition of alcoholic drinks means that L’Etage assumes bar status in the evenings.
